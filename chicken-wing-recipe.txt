Ingredients:
 - Chicken Wings
 - 1/2 cup Frank's Red Hot Sauce
 - 1/4 cup Mexican Hot Sauce or other hot sauce
 - 1/4 stick Butter
 
Directions:
 1. Bake wings on a parchment paper covered pan at 350 degrees for 1.5 hours.
 2. Add butter chopped up to a mixing bowl.
 3. Add the hot wings to the bowl and stir while their heat melts the butter.
 4. Add hot sauces and stir until blended with butter and covering the wings.
 5. Eat
